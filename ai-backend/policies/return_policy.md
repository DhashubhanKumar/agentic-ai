# Chronos Luxury Watches - Return & Refund Policy

## Eligibility Criteria

### 1. **Time Window**
- Returns must be initiated within **7 days** of delivery date
- Refund requests must be submitted within **10 days** of delivery

### 2. **Product Condition**
- Watch must be in **unworn, pristine condition**
- All original packaging, tags, and documentation must be intact
- No scratches, marks, or signs of wear
- Protective film/stickers must still be attached

### 3. **Proof of Purchase**
- Valid order ID required
- Original payment method must be verifiable

### 4. **Reason for Return**
- Defective/damaged on arrival
- Wrong item shipped
- Changed mind (subject to restocking fee)
- Size/fit issues
- Not as described

### 5. **Non-Returnable Items**
- Custom/engraved watches
- Watches with broken seals or tampered warranty cards
- Items purchased during final sale events

### 6. **Return Shipping**
- Customer responsible for return shipping costs (except defective items)
- Must use insured, trackable shipping method
- Chronos not liable for items lost in return transit

## Refund Process Timeline
- **Inspection**: 2-3 business days after receiving returned item
- **Refund Processing**: 5-7 business days after approval
- **Credit to Account**: Depends on payment provider (typically 3-10 business days)

## Restocking Fee
- 15% restocking fee applies to "changed mind" returns
- No fee for defective or incorrect items
